Consulting

High Tide provides expert consulting to help Connecticut businesses develop and implement IT asset disposition strategies that meet compliance requirements and maximize value recovery. We assess your current processes, identify gaps, and design a comprehensive plan tailored to your organization's needs.

Whether you need guidance on NIST 800-88 compliance, data security policies, or downstream vendor management, our 25+ years of industry experience ensure you receive practical, actionable recommendations.

Logistics

We manage every aspect of the logistics chain so you do not have to. Our comprehensive logistics services include:

  • Pick-Up: Scheduled on-site pickup from your location anywhere in Connecticut and the Northeast
  • Shipping: Secure, GPS-tracked transport with chain-of-custody documentation
  • Weighing: Accurate weight certification for all materials processed
  • Storage: Secure, monitored warehousing for assets awaiting processing or remarketing

Data Security

Data security is at the core of everything we do. Every asset that enters our facility is tracked with a complete chain of custody from the moment of pickup through final disposition. We follow NIST 800-88 guidelines for media sanitization, and every data-bearing device receives a detailed Certificate of Destruction documenting serial numbers, destruction methods, and timestamps.

Our processes support compliance with HIPAA, SOX, PCI-DSS, FACTA, GLBA, and other regulatory frameworks. Your auditors will have exactly the documentation they need.

Billing

High Tide offers transparent, straightforward billing with no hidden fees. We provide detailed asset inventories and clear pricing before any work begins. Equipment with remaining market value is refurbished and remarketed, generating revenue that offsets your disposition costs.

Our goal is to deliver the highest-possible margins to our clients while maintaining the highest standards of data security and environmental compliance. Many clients find that value recovery from remarketing significantly reduces or even eliminates their ITAD costs.
