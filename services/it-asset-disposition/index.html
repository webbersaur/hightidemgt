What Is IT Asset Disposition?

IT asset disposition (ITAD) is the systematic process of safely disposing of obsolete or unwanted IT equipment in a way that protects sensitive data, complies with environmental regulations, and recovers maximum value. According to NIST Special Publication 800-88, proper media sanitization is a critical component of any IT asset disposition program. High Tide delivers IT asset disposition Connecticut businesses need to meet these requirements with confidence.

Our ITAD Services

High Tide provides end-to-end IT asset disposition Connecticut businesses depend on, covering every phase of the asset retirement lifecycle:

  • Equipment recovery: Secure pickup and transportation of retired IT assets from your location
  • Asset tracking: Comprehensive inventory management with serial number documentation and chain-of-custody records
  • Data sanitization: Certified data destruction using NIST 800-88 compliant methods ensuring complete data security
  • Remarketing: Maximize value through certified refurbishment and resale programs for equipment with remaining useful life
  • Recycling: Environmentally responsible processing of non-reusable equipment through certified e-waste recycling
  • Compliance documentation: Certificates of destruction and recycling for audit purposes and regulatory requirements

The ITAD Process

Our structured IT asset disposition process ensures nothing falls through the cracks:

  1. Assessment: We evaluate your retiring assets to determine the best disposition path for each item
  2. Secure collection: Professional pickup with asset tagging and chain-of-custody documentation
  3. Data destruction: All storage devices undergo certified sanitization before any further processing
  4. Asset evaluation: Equipment is tested and graded for remarketing potential
  5. Disposition: Assets are routed to remarketing, recycling, or destruction based on condition and value
  6. Reporting: You receive comprehensive disposition reports with certificates for every asset

Value Recovery and Remarketing

One of the key benefits of professional IT asset disposition is value recovery. Many retired IT assets still have significant market value. High Tide's remarketing program identifies equipment suitable for refurbishment and resale, generating revenue that can offset your ITAD program costs. Our team stays current on secondary market values for servers, networking equipment, laptops, and enterprise storage to ensure you receive fair returns.

Compliance and Regulatory Requirements

Proper IT asset disposition Connecticut businesses need must satisfy increasingly strict data protection and environmental regulations. High Tide's ITAD services are designed to meet:

  • HIPAA: Healthcare data protection requirements
  • SOX: Sarbanes-Oxley financial data security mandates
  • GLBA: Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act for financial institutions
  • FACTA: Fair and Accurate Credit Transactions Act disposal rules
  • PCI-DSS: Payment Card Industry data security standards
  • Connecticut DEEP: State environmental disposal regulations
