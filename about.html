Connecticut's Trusted ITAD & Data Destruction Company

Our Story So Far

High Tide Commodities Management boasts more than 25 years of experience in IT asset disposition and recycling solutions. Led by Peter Goldenberg, the company emphasizes complete transparency, the highest-possible margins, and comprehensive back-end processing for end-of-life electronics and metals.

We serve Connecticut and the Northeast, working with Fortune 500 companies, healthcare systems, financial institutions, government agencies, and smaller businesses that need secure, compliant IT asset retirement.

Who We Are

High Tide Commodities Management is a fully integrated IT asset disposition provider based in Branford, Connecticut, specializing in certified data destruction and responsible electronic recycling. We combine industry expertise with personalized service to deliver results that protect your data, your bottom line, and the environment.

What We Do

Our comprehensive suite of ITAD services covers every phase of IT asset retirement:

  • IT Asset Disposition — end-to-end management of surplus and end-of-life IT equipment
  • Data Destruction — NIST 800-88 compliant sanitization including shredding, degaussing, and software wiping
  • Asset Serialization — detailed inventory and tracking of every asset by serial number
  • Destruction Witnessing — on-site observation of data destruction for maximum accountability
  • Data Center Decommissioning — complete project management from assessment through final certification
  • Equipment Remarketing — refurbishment and resale to recover maximum value from retired assets
  • Chain-of-Custody Documentation — full compliance support for HIPAA, SOX, and PCI-DSS audits

Why We Are the Best

Connecticut businesses choose High Tide because we deliver what matters most — security, transparency, and compliance:

  • Real-Time Visibility and Tracking — complete insight into your assets from pickup to final disposition
  • NIST 800-88 Guidelines — every data destruction engagement follows the gold standard for media sanitization
  • Documented Chain-of-Custody — GPS-tracked transport and serialized asset records at every stage
  • Audit-Ready Certification Documentation — Certificates of Destruction and compliance reports your auditors require
