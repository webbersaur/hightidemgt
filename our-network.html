Our Network

Experience, attention to detail and a professional team define us.

High Tide Commodities Management maintains a carefully vetted network of certified partners and vendors to ensure the highest standards of responsible electronics recycling, data security, and environmental compliance. Every partner in our network meets rigorous certification requirements and undergoes regular audits to maintain their standing.

R2 Certified Electronics Recycling

High Tide partners exclusively with R2 certified vendors for electronics recycling, ensuring your retired IT assets are processed to the highest environmental and data security standards. The R2 Standard, developed by Sustainable Electronics Recycling International (SERI), is the leading certification for electronics recyclers worldwide.

Our R2 certified partners undergo rigorous third-party audits that verify compliance with responsible recycling practices, environmental health and safety requirements, and data security protocols. This certification provides our clients with confidence that their assets are handled responsibly from collection through final disposition.

Key benefits of our R2 certified network:

  • Chain-of-custody documentation tracking materials from pickup to final processing
  • Compliance support for HIPAA, SOX, PCI-DSS, and other regulatory frameworks
  • Regular third-party audits ensuring ongoing adherence to R2 standards
  • Environmental accountability with documented downstream vendor management
  • Data security protocols meeting or exceeding NIST 800-88 guidelines

ISO Certified Vendors

Our vendor network maintains multiple ISO certifications that demonstrate commitment to quality, environmental responsibility, and information security:

  • ISO 9001 (Quality Management): Ensures consistent, high-quality processes for handling, processing, and recycling IT assets. ISO 9001 certified vendors follow documented procedures with measurable quality objectives and continuous improvement practices.
  • ISO 14001 (Environmental Management): Verifies that our partners operate effective environmental management systems, minimizing the environmental impact of electronics recycling operations and maintaining compliance with environmental regulations.
  • ISO 27001 (Information Security): Confirms that our vendors maintain comprehensive information security management systems to protect sensitive data throughout the asset disposition process. This certification is critical for clients in healthcare, finance, and government sectors.

All ISO certified vendors in our network undergo regular surveillance audits to maintain their certifications, ensuring that standards are not just met at the time of certification but maintained continuously throughout our partnership.
