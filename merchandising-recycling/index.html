Large-Scale Retail Merchandising Recycling: A Sustainable Solution for Your Displays

Comprehensive recycling solutions for retail merchandising materials, point-of-purchase displays, and promotional fixtures.

High Tide Commodities Management provides comprehensive large-scale retail merchandising recycling solutions designed to help retailers, brands, and merchandising companies meet their sustainability goals. From reverse logistics coordination to final material processing, we manage every step of the merchandising recycling process so you can focus on your next campaign.

The Challenge of Retail Merchandising Waste

Retail merchandising generates enormous volumes of waste with every seasonal refresh, product launch, and promotional campaign. The challenge lies in the complexity of these materials:

  • Mixed materials: Modern retail displays combine plastics, metals, wood, cardboard, fabric, and electronics into single units that require careful separation for proper recycling
  • Varying sizes: From countertop displays to full floor-standing fixtures, merchandising materials come in every shape and size, complicating collection and transport
  • Logistical complexity: National and regional retailers need coordinated collection across dozens or hundreds of store locations, each with different access requirements and schedules
  • Electronic components: Digital signage, LED lighting, and interactive displays contain electronic components that require specialized e-waste handling

Our Comprehensive Recycling Solutions

De-installation and Collection Coordination

We coordinate the removal and collection of merchandising materials from retail locations across Connecticut and the Northeast. Our team manages scheduling, transportation logistics, and chain-of-custody documentation from each store location to our Branford processing facility.

Sorting and Material Separation

At our facility, trained technicians disassemble and sort merchandising materials by type. This careful separation process ensures each material stream is directed to the most appropriate recycling pathway, maximizing recovery rates and minimizing waste.

Responsible Recycling and Upcycling

Every material recovered from retail displays is processed through the appropriate recycling channel:

  • Plastics are cleaned, shredded, and processed into pellets or flakes for use in new products
  • Metals (steel, aluminum, copper) are recovered, separated, and melted down for reuse
  • Cardboard and paper are baled and sent to paper mills for recycling
  • Wood is chipped for biomass fuel, mulch, or composite material manufacturing
  • Electronics are routed to our certified e-waste recycling partners for responsible processing

Reporting and Compliance

We provide detailed recycling reports documenting material weights, diversion rates, and disposal methods. This documentation supports your corporate sustainability reporting, helps you meet ESG targets, and demonstrates compliance with state and federal waste regulations.
