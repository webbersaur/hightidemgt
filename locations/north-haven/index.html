The I-91 Corridor Business Profile

North Haven sits at a strategic logistics intersection — I-91, Route 5, and rail access have made it home to a heavy concentration of distribution operations, light manufacturing, and corporate offices. Major employers like Medtronic, ASML's regional presence, and a long roster of mid-market manufacturers operate substantial server rooms, networking infrastructure, and fleets of workstations. When that infrastructure refreshes — or when a facility consolidates — the IT retirement scope can run from a single server rack to a multi-floor data center decommissioning.

Server Decommissioning in North Haven

When a North Haven data center, server room, or colo space is being shut down, the project has more moving parts than a typical IT refresh: physical rack removal, network drop disconnection, UPS handling, asset reconciliation, and (most importantly) certified destruction of every drive that ever held production data. We handle the entire workflow as a managed project — typically scoped on a site visit, then executed across a single coordinated weekend or planned multi-day window.
