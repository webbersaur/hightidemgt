IT Asset Disposition & Data Destruction in New Haven, CT

Secure ITAD, NIST 800-88 certified data destruction, and e-waste recycling via R2 certified downstream partners for New Haven businesses, Yale, biotech firms, and healthcare systems.

High Tide Commodities Management provides IT asset disposition, data destruction, and electronic recycling services for New Haven, Connecticut. Our Branford headquarters is twelve minutes east on I-95, which means we deliver true local service to one of Connecticut's most data-sensitive business environments — Yale University, the Yale New Haven Health system, the Science Park biotech cluster, and the city's downtown professional services corridor.

Serving a Data-Sensitive Market

New Haven's economy concentrates more high-stakes data handling than almost anywhere else in the state. Yale alone runs research computing infrastructure across dozens of departments. Yale New Haven Hospital and Smilow Cancer Hospital process patient records governed by HIPAA. Alexion, BioHaven, and the cluster of biotech firms on College Street and around Science Park generate research data subject to FDA, EMA, and corporate IP protections. Downtown financial services firms handle records under SOX, GLBA, and FACTA. Every one of these organizations periodically retires IT equipment — and every retirement is a data-leak risk if not handled properly.

Why New Haven Organizations Choose High Tide

HIPAA & research data expertise. We've worked extensively with healthcare and life sciences clients in the region. Our processes are designed around documented chain-of-custody and audit-ready certificates — the kind your compliance officer, IRB, or sponsor auditors will want to see.

On-site destruction. For sensitive Yale research projects, hospital IT refreshes, or biotech equipment retirements, we bring industrial-grade shredding directly to your facility. Your team witnesses every drive destroyed before it leaves the building — eliminating the chain-of-custody risk window entirely.

Responsible recycling. Equipment that doesn't contain sensitive data still needs responsible end-of-life processing. Every monitor, server, switch, and component moves through R2v3-certified downstream channels - no informal export, no landfill. Read about what R2 covers.

Local accountability. National ITAD providers ship your equipment across state lines. We process it twelve minutes from your door, at our Branford facility. If you have a question about an asset, you can call the same number that picked it up.
