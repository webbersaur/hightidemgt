Serving Madison's Local Economy

Madison's economy is built on small and family-owned businesses, professional service firms, hospitality and marine industries along the Sound, and shoreline retail. The Madison Town Green and Boston Post Road host law and accounting offices, real estate agencies, medical practices, and a healthy small-business community. With Hammonasset Beach State Park drawing seasonal traffic and a residential base that skews higher-income, the IT refresh and e-waste needs here are steady — but most local providers either treat Madison as an afterthought or charge a premium for shoreline pickup. We don't.
