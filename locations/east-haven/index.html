Serving East Haven's Working-Class Shoreline

East Haven's character is shaped by its working-class shoreline economy — small businesses along Main Street and Coe Avenue, marine and waterfront operations near the Farm River and the Sound, automotive and trade services, and a mix of professional offices serving the local community. The town's proximity to Tweed New Haven Airport and I-95 means logistics and small-fleet operations are well represented too. These businesses don't always need enterprise-scale ITAD — but they do need a way to get rid of old computers, retired monitors, and aging hard drives without dropping them in a dumpster or worrying about a data breach.
